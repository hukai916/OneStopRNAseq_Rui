How to interpret GSEA results
Gene Sets for Human and Mouse: MSigDB
Gene Sets for Other Species: msigdbr


Quick Tips:
  • Start browsing results from index.html under each subfolder (e.g. gsea/KO_D8_vs_KO_D0/m1.all.v2022.1.Mm.symbols.gmt.GseaPreranked/index.html)
  • NES (Normalized Enrichment Score), FDR q-val, and the Enrichment plot are very helpful in interpreting GSEA results
